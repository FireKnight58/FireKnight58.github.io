During my time snow skiing I broke my legs... twice. While at Wilson high school I joined the Snow Ski Race team. my skill after raised rappidly and I now find any run short of a black dimond (that is a ski run that is rated to be hard, but not very hard) to be almost boaring.



My dad joined a water skiing show team called Portland Water spetacular. when I was born I became a member of the team by default. We often do shows for crowds in areas like West Linn, Lake Oswego, and other lakes and rivers.
